About Us

The Association of Christian Christites (ACC) is a thriving community at Christ (Deemed to Be University), uniting over 5,000 Christian students. We aim to spread the good news, fostering fellowship and a positive campus environment. By organizing events like Leadership Training programs, Discipleship Training Programme, Bible Fest, Retreats, Outreach initiatives, Holy Mass, and Christmas celebrations, we strive to help students lead fulfilling Christian lives. Additionally, ACC offers various domains like Animation, Audio Visual, Media & Documentation, Liturgy, and Outreach, allowing members to glorify God through their unique talents.
