    About Us

    Know Us

    Association of Christian Christites is a community of all Christian students studying in Christ (Deemed To Be University). We spread the message of Jesus, encourage fellowship, and support each other in leading fulfilling Christian lives, striving to make a meaningful difference in the world.

    ACC creates a strong bond of friendship and community among students. Our core vision is to build a supportive, faith-filled atmosphere on campus that glorifies God. We organize regular activities, including Holy Mass, Leadership Training Programmes, spiritual retreats, cultural fests, feast celebrations, outreach initiatives, and daily intercessory prayers. The ACC community includes over 4,500 members spanning across five campuses of Christ (Deemed To Be University).

    ACC operates through six specialized domains that allow members to apply their unique talents: Animation, Audio & Visual, Liturgy, Logistics & Hospitality, Media & Documentation, and Outreach. Each domain is managed by a dedicated student volunteer body.

    Every day, members of ACC meet at the St. Kuriakose Chapel to pray together for our university, the student community, the world, and special intentions. Additionally, a monthly Holy Mass is organized on the First Friday of every month at the chapel, prepared cooperatively by students from various academic departments.
